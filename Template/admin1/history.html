Important Milestones

  • 2019

    Diamond Jubilee celebrations started. New Sports Complex with a total built-up area of 7,491 sqm inaugurated. Direct Admission of Students Abroad (DASA) coordinated.

  • 2018

    HEFA grant to strengthen Central Research Facility, and infrastructural development fund received. New academic and residential buildings about 54,840 sqm inaugurated (CSE, Chemical, Applied Mechanics, Basic Sciences, Library, Staff quarters and Girls hostel).

  • 2017

    Lecture Hall Complex with a total built-up area of about 8,193 sqm. Vertical extension of new Mechanical Engineering block (4th, 5th and 6th floors) with a total built-up area of about 6,355 sqm inaugurated. TEQIP-III commences.

  • 2016

    Centralized counselling for M.Tech/M.Arch/M. Plan (CCMT) coordinated by the Institute. Installation of total 1MWp (1000KWp) Capacity SolarPower Plants at the rooftop of 18 existing buildings on RESCO model of the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy through Solar Energy Corporation of India

  • 2015

    Mentored IIIT Dharwad entrusted by MoE.

  • 2014

    All the UG programmes got accredited for five years - Direct Admission of Students Direct Admission of Students Abroad (DASA) coordinated for five continuous years (2010-2014)

  • 2012

    New ladies hostel with 347 occupants inaugurated.

  • 2011

    New boys mega hostel with 1512 occupant inaugurated.
